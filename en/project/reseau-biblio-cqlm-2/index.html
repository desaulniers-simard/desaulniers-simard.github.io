Réseau BIBLIO Centre-du-Québec, Lanaudière et Mauricie Information Center

Réseau BIBLIO CQLM provides operational support and software access to small municipalities’ libraries. The Information Center serves as a launchpad to all work applications, their documentation, Réseau news and activities. Access is granted to the extranet thanks to Active Directory integration.
